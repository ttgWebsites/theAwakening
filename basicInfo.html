Basic Info

  • Title
  • The Awakening
  • Author
  • Kate Chopin
  • Published
  • United States, Herbert S. Stone and Co. 1899.
  • Genre
  • Bildungsroman
  • Setting
  • The novel is set in 1899 at the beginning of the industrial revolution and feminist movement. The beginning of the novel is set in Grand Isle, where many Creoles from New Orleans vacation, while the rest of the novel is set in the French Quarter in New Orleans.
  • Point Of View
  • The novel is written in the third person by an unspecified narrator.
  • Conflict
  • As Edna begins a quest for independence, she realizes that she is at odds with societies expectations for women. She struggles to understand why she must give up her own goals and needs for her husband and children.
